Hi, I'm Laura Kentcroft.

Gameplay · Systems · Technical · Player Experience

I design player-facing systems that are creative, readable and technically achievable. My background spans gameplay programming, multiplayer systems, Unreal implementation, UI/UX, LiveOps, economy design, feature ownership and cross-discipline collaboration across indie, AA and AAA projects.

I’m especially interested in the space between design intent, technical implementation and player experience - turning complex ideas into systems that feel clear, fair and satisfying to play.
